WHAT IS RACETRACK COACH?

Our motto: Work with the best

The online platform of RacetrackCoach GmbH provides motorsport enthusiasts and instructors with an innovative framework for a simple and successful cooperation. The right expert for every task: In this sense, the official "Racetrack Coach" app sets standards, and sees itself as an online interface for ambitious amateur racers and professional racers.

RacetrackCoach GmbH, based in Bochum, Germany, not only has the largest, most up-to-date race track database, but also the largest profile database of its own professional racers and instructors in Germany, Belgium, Finland and the USA. The official "Racetrack Coach" smartphone app offers users a variety of services – from hotel search to racetrack analysis and tools for optimal preparation for your upcoming day at the racetrack.

Available in the App Store for Android and iOs!

Racing drivers development program

We build on our own racers. With the expertise and support of our current racers & coaches, we offer our development drivers exactly what every racer needs at the start of their career: complete assistance in terms of marketing, fitness, but also in the search and choice of the next cockpit, we work in the spirit of our development drivers.

Weekly updates on the comparison of physical training with the support of "Iron Man" triathletes, so that our development riders never neglect the necessary fitness training, as well as collaborative sim racing among colleagues are part of the program of the racing driver development program of Racetrack Coach GmbH.

» Inquiry

We strive for a long-term, common future for our development drivers. In order to make this possible, we offer the opportunity to make a mutual decision on a possible common future after the first three months of cooperation.

We want to provide every single one of our development drivers with optimal, individual advice. Therefore, our capacity is limited. Please contact Racetrack Coach GmbH in any way to find out if a place in the racing driver development program would be free. Coaching is in English.

Sim Racing

The RTC setups developed by our professional racers bring you a whole step closer to the podium! With the latest analysis software and engineering knowledge from real motorsport, it is now possible to create excellent virtual setups. Maybe this setup only partially fits your driving style, but now you can completely focus on driving! Use PRO hints in our smartphone app as a target and develop your own understanding. For cars with a lot of power combined with less downforce or without slick tires, you may need to set earlier braking points. Have fun and success on the track!
